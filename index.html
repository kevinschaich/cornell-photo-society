About Us

We take the best pictures on campus.

The Cornell Photo Society (CPS) is Cornell’s foremost organization dedicated to the art of photography. We welcome people with a wide range of skills and experience in photography, from beginner to advanced. We function as a hub for photographers to network, collaborate, and improve together. Every week we delve into different types of photography through workshops and outings. Join us as we explore astrophotography, timelapses, high-speed imagery, and aerial photography.
